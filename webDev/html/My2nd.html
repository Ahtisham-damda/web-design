New Delhi: India and Sri Lanka will carry out a 12-day mega military exercise from Monday.
The eight edition of the ''Mitra Shakti'' exercise will be conducted at Combat Training School in Sri Lanka's Ampara from 4 to 15 October.
"The aim of the exercise is to promote close relations between armies of both countries and enhance inter-operability.
