Kurulus osman

urulus Osman is a popular Turkish series that focuses on the life of Osman Bey, the son of Ertugrul Ghazi and the founder of the Ottoman Empire. ... Fans of the series were left disappointed after reports surfaced that Engin will not be reprising his role as Ertugrul Bey in the sequel titled "Kurulus Osman".

Season two of Kuruluş: Osman is also the seventh season of the two Turkish TV series when including the seasons of Diriliş: Ertuğrul and the second season of the historical drama television series. It premiered on 7 October 2020 and finished on 23 June 2021.

Bala Hatun is played by the actress Özge Törer. She made her debut on 20th November 2019, which was the first episode of Kurulus Osman. Since then, she has appeared in all of the episodes to date. She plays a significant role in this show, as she is the wife of Osman Ghazi.

Turgut is portrayed by Turkish actor Cengiz Coşkun and the character is based on one of the companions of Ertuğrul, the father of Osman I.
