HTML Entities

Use: mainly used for special symbols that are not present in keyboard

pattern &entity_name; or &#entity_number;

Examples

Blank Space ->   there is extra space between a   b

Copyright Symbol -> © OR ©

Trademark Symbol -> ™ OR ™

Registered Symbol -> ® OR ®

Pound Symbol -> £ OR £

Euro Symbol -> € OR €

Rupee Symbol -> ₹
